Tequila Chilli

Description

This simple and quick one-pot chili

has great flavor and is nice and meaty.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Breaking it up with a spoon while it cooks; drain.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, kidney beans, onion, garlic, tomato sauce, tequila, chili powder, cumin, salt, and cayenne pepper.
  4. Simmer 20 minutes.
